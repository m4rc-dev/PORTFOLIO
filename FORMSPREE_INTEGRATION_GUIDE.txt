FORMSPREE INTEGRATION GUIDE FOR YOUR PORTFOLIO

1. Sign up for a free Formspree account:
   - Go to https://formspree.io
   - Click "Get Started Free"
   - Enter your email and create an account
   - Verify your email address

2. Create a new form:
   - After logging in, click "New Project" or "+ New Form"
   - Give your form a name (e.g., "Portfolio Contact Form")
   - Enter the email where you want to receive messages
   - Click "Create Form"

3. Get your Form ID:
   - After creating the form, you'll see a page with integration instructions
   - Look for your Form ID in the URL or in the integration code
   - It will look something like "f/xyz12345" where "xyz12345" is your actual Form ID

4. Update your HTML form:
   - Open index.html in your portfolio
   - Find the form element with class "contact-form"
   - Replace "YOUR_FORM_ID" in the action attribute with your actual Form ID
   - The action should look like: action="https://formspree.io/f/xyz12345"

5. Test your form:
   - Save all changes
   - Open your portfolio in a browser
   - Fill out and submit the contact form
   - Check your email for the submission (it may take a few minutes)

That's it! Your contact form will now send messages directly to your email through Formspree's free service.

LIMITATIONS OF THE FREE PLAN:
- 50 submissions per form per month
- Basic spam protection
- No custom redirect URLs
- No file attachments

If you need more features, Formspree offers paid plans starting at $10/month.
